My name is Christopher Chapline. I'm a junior in the Computer Science department at the University of Arizona. My project idea is called UA Friend Scheduler

UA Friend Scheduler is a web application that will allow for people to quickly and easily determine who is currently available to meet up throughout the day. Each user will enter their class, work, obligation schedule. Upon connecting with other members who have done the same, throughout the day, the user's calendar will show various connections who are currently available. This makes scheduling meetings, dates, meet-ups, and get togethers much easier!

The current list of desired features follows:

  • The ability for users to login
  • The ability for users to input their various obligations into the application
  • The ability for users to view their friend's schedule and availability
  • The ability for users to schedule meet-ups in advance with their friends
  • The ability to create groups that can schedule group meetings based on the availability of their members
